Our Solutions

Four ways to work with us.

Each one is designed around where your organisation is right now — and where you want to get to. Most clients begin with training and grow from there. Some come ready to build. Either way, we work with you closely at every stage.

Where Most Organisations Begin

A structured programme that shows your team what AI can actually do — inside your type of work.

This is not a generic AI literacy course. Before we design anything, we spend time understanding your team's current skill level, the function they work in, and what you are trying to achieve. The programme is built around that — not around a fixed curriculum.

On Duration
No Fixed Timeline

No two programmes run for the same length of time. The duration depends entirely on three things — the current AI maturity of the team coming in, the depth of coverage required across modules, and the outcomes the organisation wants to walk away with.

A focused team with some prior exposure may complete a high-impact programme in 2 concentrated days. A larger, mixed-maturity team working across multiple functions might run the same content as a part-time programme spread over 6 to 8 weeks — allowing time for practice and reflection between sessions.

We scope the duration after our initial discovery conversation. What does not change is what the programme is built around — your team, your workflows, and your outcomes.

Building the Habit, Not Just the Knowledge

We stay with you for 4 to 6 months as your team implements, refines, and embeds AI into how they work.

Most organisations hit a wall between the workshop and actual adoption. The training lands well. The workshop produces good ideas. Then the team goes back to the day-to-day, priorities take over, and momentum slows. This tier exists to prevent that.

The Four Phases

Click any phase to expand its detail.

Phase 1 Weeks 1 – 3
Implementation Start
We help the team put the workshop outputs into live operation — real systems, real workflows, running for real.

This is where the prototype becomes production. We sit with your team as they begin using the systems built in the workshop, troubleshoot what is not working in the real environment, and begin identifying which new areas should be prioritised next. The goal by the end of this phase: the core workflow is live and the team is using it daily.

Phase 2 Weeks 4 – 8
Refinement & Troubleshooting
We fix what is not working. We identify new opportunities as the team gets comfortable.

Reality always surfaces edge cases the workshop did not anticipate. In this phase we address them. We also begin expanding the team's usage — identifying adjacent workflows where AI can add value now that the core systems are stable. Fortnightly working sessions with the team continue throughout.

Phase 3 Weeks 9 – 16
Team Capability Build
We shift from doing together to coaching your team to run it themselves.

The goal of this phase is independence. We begin handing over ownership of the systems to your team — they build, we review. We introduce more complex use cases as confidence grows. This is also the phase where the need for a custom-built solution often becomes apparent, and where the conversation about Tier 4 begins naturally if relevant.

Phase 4 Weeks 17 – 24
Review & Handover
We step back. Your team runs this independently.

A structured review of everything built and embedded over the engagement. We document what is running, provide your team with a clear maintenance guide, and formally step back from active involvement. The handover is a genuine transfer of ownership — not a formality. Your team runs this from here.

What We Actually Do During This Period

We are not available for occasional questions. We are actively involved.

Fortnightly working sessions with the team — reviewing what is working, what is not, and what to build or improve next. Not check-in calls. Actual working sessions.

Direct support when automations break, outputs drift, or a new use case needs to be designed — without waiting for the next scheduled session.

Continuous expansion of the AI toolset as the team's confidence and capability grows — new tools, new use cases, new automations added progressively.

Identification of where a custom-built solution would create more value than off-the-shelf tools — this is often where the natural conversation about Tier 4 begins.

Outcome

By the end of the engagement, AI is not a tool your team occasionally uses — it is embedded in how they work. The workflows are running. The habits are built. Your team understands the system well enough to maintain and extend it without us.

Best For

Organisations serious about making AI a permanent part of their operation — not a one-time initiative. Leadership that recognises that change at team level takes time and structured support to actually stick.

From Workflow to Infrastructure

When the need goes beyond what off-the-shelf tools can solve, we design, build, and deploy a custom AI system — and hand it over to your team to run.

Most organisations that reach this stage have been through training and hand-holding with us. They know what AI can do. They know where their biggest opportunity is. And they have reached the point where the solution they need cannot be assembled from existing tools — it needs to be built.

This is where we bring in our engineering capability. We work closely with your team throughout the build. By the time we deploy, your people understand the system, can manage it day to day, and know how to develop it further.

How a Build Engagement Works

Four phases. No black-box delivery at the end.

Regular check-ins with your team throughout every phase. Click to expand each stage.

We sit with your team and map the exact problem we are solving. What data does the system need access to? What does a good output look like? Where does the system sit inside your existing operations? What does your team need to be able to do themselves once it is live?

No assumptions. No standard templates. We design from your context — every architectural decision is grounded in how your business actually operates.

Our AI-first engineering team builds the system. We use the right tools for the job — OpenAI and Anthropic APIs for LLM capability, workflow orchestration tools for automation, and custom code where needed.

We build for maintainability — clean, documented, and designed so your team can manage it without needing to come back to us for every change. Regular check-ins with your team throughout. No surprises at the end.

Your team tests the system against real scenarios from your actual operation. We refine based on what comes back — this is where edge cases get handled and the system gets genuinely ready for live operation. Not demo-ready. Actually ready.

We stay in close contact throughout this phase and move quickly on refinements. No back-and-forth over weeks.

We deploy. We run a structured handover session with your team — not a one-hour walkthrough, but a working session where your people operate the system themselves with us in the room. We document everything. We stay available for a post-launch support window.

After handover, you own it. The code, the system, the data. All of it. No licensing arrangement. No ongoing dependency on Nexivant to keep it running.
